# TripMind AI - Business Brief (Funding Ready)

## Vision
TripMind AI makes trip planning reliable, fast, and budget-safe by shifting from "suggestions" to a hard-constraint decision system.

## Problem
Current travel apps often:
- Suggest destinations users cannot afford
- Ignore realistic travel-distance constraints
- Overwhelm users with generic recommendations
- Convert poorly because recommendations lack feasibility trust

## Our Solution
TripMind AI enforces strict feasibility before recommending anything:
- Distance filters by trip duration
- Budget hard-limits (including transport share caps)
- Cost simulation before output
- Immediate rejection for non-feasible destinations

Result: more trust, better conversion, lower drop-off.

## Product Highlights
- Constraint-first recommendation engine
- AI-assisted conversational onboarding
- Weather and crowd context
- Strict cost breakdown for decision confidence
- Saved trips, lead capture, itinerary upsell, admin analytics

## Differentiation (Moat)
1. **Constraint Engine** over generic LLM output
2. **Feasibility-first UX** with transparent cost breakdown
3. **Psychology + budget + distance** unified flow
